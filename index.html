WHAT IS THE 13TH ADMENDENT?

In, 1865 the goal of the 13th was to abolish slavery. However, prisoners are excluded from this due to a clause stating that slavery is only acceptable "as a punishment for crime whereof the party shall have been duly convicted."

So this allows prisons/ brands to exploit prisoners...
